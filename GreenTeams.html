Green Teams at De Plantage

Our Green Teams play a vital role in spreading awareness and inspiring environmental action among colleagues. Responsibilities include:

  • Informing colleagues about new environmental practices.
  • Motivating and inspiring everyone to contribute to environmental initiatives.
  • Engaging in activities such as sorting and collecting recyclables.
