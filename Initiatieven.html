Our Initiatives

De Plantage is actively involved in several environmental initiatives, including:

  1. Recycling garbage to minimize environmental impact.
  2. Purchasing only recycled products such as paper, coffee cups, etc.
  3. Using 100% renewable energy from Greenchoice to power our operations.
