About The Edge

The Edge is a modern office building located in the Zuidas business district of Amsterdam. It is known for its innovative and sustainable design.

Features include:

  • Energy-efficient design
  • Solar panels
  • Smart LED lighting
  • Underfloor heating and cooling

Location

The Edge is situated at Gustav Mahlerlaan 2970, 1081 LA Amsterdam, Netherlands.
